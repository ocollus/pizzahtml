Welcome to Bruno Pizzeria

Located in Trang Tien street, the center of Hanoi City, Vietnam, Bruno Pizzeria has an appealing facade and a very good-looking, highlighted entrance, promised to attract any pizza lover by the first look. As you walk into the pizzeria, you will be overwhelmed by the friendly yet professional attitude of our staff team and the manager as well. The neat and clean space, the simple but stunning layout, the regularly checked and renewed furniture, the good-looking and also good-tasting pizzas, all will create an unforgettable food experience for you, our lovely customers!

About Us?
The Bruno Pizzeria was founded by Mr. Bruno, an American pizza-lover who has been in Vietnam for 10 years. In the early days, the pizzeria was just a small van selling pizza in the trunk. Three years later, through talent and experiences gathered from hard working, Mr. Bruno decided to open the whole pizzeria in the center of the capital of Vietnam. The next three years was a very hard time for Mr. Bruno because of the slightly unreasonable prices, the unstable income, the hard-to-get market tastes, but as time went by, with the help of the hospitality Vietnamese people, he managed to overcame difficulties to make the flawless pizzeria like nowadays.
